Husker Scope

Senior Design Project - Husker Scope

The Husker Scope project will be an all-in-one cross platform (IOS and Android) app that implements a Dual Channel Function Generator and Oscilloscope functionality for use within the CSCE 436 - Advanced Embedded Systems course. They will have a beta version available for students to use in the spring 2022.
